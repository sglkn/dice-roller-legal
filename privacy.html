Privacy Policy (EN)

1. Data Controller

The controller within the meaning of the GDPR is:
Rolf Seegelken, Germany
Contact: privacy@rolfseegelken.de

2. General

We take the protection of your data seriously. In general, the app can be used without you actively providing personal data.

3. What data may be processed?

Depending on your settings (consent), we may process the following categories of data:

  • Usage data: in-app events (e.g., screen views, button taps, app start)
  • Technical metadata: coarse timestamp, app version, OS version
  • Consent data: consent status, timestamp, version, banner language (via Usercentrics)

We do not send content such as messages, contacts, or photos. Our server-side tracking does not transmit stable user IDs.

4. Consent Management (Usercentrics)

We use the Usercentrics Consent Management Platform to manage user consent. The following information may be processed:

  • consent status, timestamp, consent version
  • banner language
  • technical metadata (e.g., user agent)

Legal basis: Art. 6(1)(c) GDPR.

5. Anonymous Usage Statistics (Server-side)

For technical analysis, error detection, and app improvement, we process anonymous usage statistics using our own server infrastructure and a server-side tag management system.

  • Only aggregated and anonymized event data is processed.
  • No processing of personal data, no user profiling, no cross-device tracking.
  • Data is not shared with third parties or advertising networks.
  • Processing takes place in Google Cloud within the EU (region europe-west3).

Legal basis: Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR (legitimate interest in stability, security, and improvement).
